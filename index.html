KidoHelp

We're excited to embrace new communities spreading joy through engaging activities and valuable knowledge sharing whilte paving the way for brighter futures by nurturing digital and technical literacy among the underprivileged youths, empowering them to thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of technology.

Objectives

Innovative and Accessible Learning Opportunities

Provide underprivileged children with innovative and accessible learning opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Quality IT Education

Deliver quality education in IT fields to underprivileged children using the Cubie platform.

Comprehensive Course Offerings

Focus on courses encompassing coding, mathematics, web development, mobile app development, electronics and robotics, AI, data science, and game development.

Employment Opportunities

Open avenues for employment by fostering collaborations with businesses willing to support our cause.

Program Structure

Phase 1:
Program Kickoff

  • Conduct an initial site visit to meet orphanage directors and assess infrastructure.
  • Discuss program goals with orphanage directors and plan for the upcoming workshop.
  • Include an observation and bonding period of 3-4 days a week over a 2-week period.

  • Check facilities and ensure the laptops are equipped with necessary software and tools.

  • Introduce the Cubie platform to orphanage staff and instructors.
  • Provide training on integrating Cubie into the educational curriculum.

Phase 2:
Workshop Implementation

  • Commence IT education courses with a focus on practical applications.
  • Regularly assess students' progress and understanding.

  • Spend 3-4 days a week at the orphanage over a 2-week period, engaging in bonding activities.
  • Implement the planned workshop covering the basics of IT education.

Phase 3:
Final Project Development and Internship Placement

  • Collaborate with students on a final project to apply their acquired skills.
  • Provide guidance and support during the project development phase.

  • Facilitate the placement of students into internship opportunities.
  • Provide ongoing support and mentorship during the internship period.

  • Evaluate the overall success of the program.
  • Gather feedback from students, instructors, and collaborating businesses.
  • Discuss possibilities for future expansion and improvement.

Who We Are

Kidocode is a school of technology and entrepreneurship for K-12 students. Our community is driven to rebuild the education system for the modern era by utilizing our AI-powered platform, Cubie. With over 7000 students and 3 campuses(and growing!) across Malaysia, we aim to transform the digital landscape throught education.

What is the KidoHelp program?

The KidoHelp program is an initiative by Kidocode that provides free technology education to underprivileged children. The program focuses on teaching essential digital skills like coding, artificial intelligence, and entrepreneurship to empower these children and open up future career opportunities.

Who can participate in the KidoHelp program?

The KidoHelp program is designed for children from underprivileged backgrounds who may not have access to quality educational resources. The program aims to support these children by offering free, high-quality training in various technology fields.

How is the KidoHelp program delivered?

KidoHelp offers both online and in-person classes, depending on the accessibility for participants. The program includes live sessions, flexible timing, and a student-centric curriculum that encourages hands-on learning and creativity.

What subjects are taught in the KidoHelp program?

The KidoHelp program covers a wide range of subjects including coding, artificial intelligence, robotics, and entrepreneurship. The curriculum is designed to be engaging and relevant, preparing children with the skills they need for the future.

Do you provide additional support?

Chat and email support is available 24/7. Phone lines are open during normal business hours.
